# Plans and storage limits

Nimbus offers three plans: Free (15 GB), Plus (2 TB) at $10/month, and Pro (10 TB) at $25/month. You can change plans anytime under Settings > Plan; downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.

Downgrading to a plan smaller than your current usage is blocked until you remove enough files to fit. Nimbus never deletes files automatically.
