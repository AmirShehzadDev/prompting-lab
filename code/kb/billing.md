# Billing and refunds

Nimbus plans are billed monthly to the card on file. You can see every charge under Settings > Billing > Order history. Duplicate charges are occasionally caused by a retried payment; they are reversed automatically within 5 business days, or you can contact support to expedite.

To update your card, go to Settings > Billing > Payment method. If a payment fails, your account is marked "past due" and you have 7 days to update payment before features are limited.

Refunds are handled case by case by a human billing agent. Support can escalate a refund request but cannot guarantee approval.
