# Sharing files and folders

To share a folder, right-click it in the Nimbus app and choose "Share", then "Create link". Set the permission to "Can view" or "Can edit" and copy the link. People with the link do not need a Nimbus account to open it in a browser.

Folders you are a member of (owned by someone else) do not count against your storage quota — only folders you own do.

To stop sharing, open the folder's Share dialog and click "Remove link". Existing links stop working immediately.
