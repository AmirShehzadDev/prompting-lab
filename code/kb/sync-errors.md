# Fixing a paused or stalled sync

If Nimbus shows "Sync paused", open the Nimbus icon in your system tray (Windows) or menu bar (macOS), click the Activity tab, and choose "Resume sync". Sync auto-pauses on metered or VPN connections unless you allow it in Settings > Network.

If sync stays stalled, check Settings > Account for a "storage full" banner — sync halts when your plan's quota is exceeded. Free up space or upgrade your plan, then resume.

Error code 0x83 means a file is locked by another app. Close the app editing the file (often Office or a PDF viewer), then click "Retry" in the Activity tab. Restarting the Nimbus app clears most transient sync errors.
